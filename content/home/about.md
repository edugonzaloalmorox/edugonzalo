# Biography

Edu is Phd student in economics at Newcastle. Her research interests include distributed robotics, mobile computing and programmable matter. She leads the Robotic Neurobiology group, which develops self-reconfiguring robots, systems of self-organizing robots, and mobile sensor networks.

Before joining Newcastle University, I worked as a researcher in european public policy.
